This is my work in progress version of BlackJack. 

In Blackjack, the objective is to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Cards are face value, face cards are 10, and aces can be 1 or 11. You get two cards and can choose to hit (take another) or stand (stop). If you go over 21, you bust. After your turn is over, the dealer reveals their cards and hits until at least a hard 17. The higher total wins, a tie is a draw or push.

More updates are to come in the future, so keep an eye out for that.
